🌡️ Temperature Converter

Convert between Celsius (°C) and Fahrenheit (°F)
📊 Celsius → Fahrenheit ➡️
°C
📊 Fahrenheit → Celsius ➡️
°F

💡 Formula: °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

🌡️ Formula: °C = (°F - 32) × 5/9

❄️ Absolute zero: -273.15 °C = -459.67 °F

📋 Common Temperature References

Water boils 100 °C = 212 °F
Body temperature 37 °C = 98.6 °F
Room temperature 20-22 °C = 68-72 °F
Water freezes 0 °C = 32 °F
Very cold -18 °C = 0 °F

Example: 100°C = 212°F | 32°F = 0°C
